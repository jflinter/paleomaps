Welcome to Paleo Maps!

Paleo maps is a fun, easy way for followers of the Paleo diet to find places to eat.

Click on any of the map markers for reviews, information, menu items, and more for paleo-friendly restaurants near you.

This site is a work in progress, with more content and functionality being added every day. If you have questions, feature suggestions, or bug reports, feel free to email them to info@paleomaps.com.
